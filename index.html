About Us

Founded in 2023, we love helping aspiring stars record music as they begin their careers in the industry. The entire experience
of kickstarting the journeys of the next generation of artists is so important to us. Come tour our brand new studio in Medford,
and check out its modern recording equipment, luxurious offices, and (of course) the delicious food selection.
Our team includes experienced musicians, production artists, marketers, and graphic designers.
We go the extra mile to make sure that your music both sounds amazing and is enjoyed by as many people as possible.
